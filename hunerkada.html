Hunerkada - Center of Excellence for Art & Culture

Prof. Dr. Hassan Sher

Vice Chancellor, University of Swat

I am delighted to announce the launch of the Hunerkada Center of Excellence at the University of Swat, marking a significant milestone in our journey towards academic excellence. Initially, we are offering two bachelor's programs: Bachelor of Fine Arts and Bachelor of Design.

The establishment of Hunerkada Center of Excellence is a testament to our commitment to fostering creativity, innovation, and artistic expression. This center will serve as a hub for art lovers, providing a platform for them to explore their talents and reach new heights.

I extend my warmest congratulations to the entire team of Hunerkada and staff who have worked tirelessly to make this vision a reality. I also welcome our new students who will be joining us on this exciting journey.

Prof. Syed Jamal Shah

Chairman, Hunerkada, Islamabad ‚Äî Eminent Artist, Scholar & Educationist

As custodians of the most enviable cultural heritage from Mehergarh to our living culture, we consider art & culture a necessity that enables people to engage with their milieu in an intimate relationship which is both creative and critical.

The intimacy of this holistic engagement results in authentic knowledge, clarity, empowerment and informed decision making. HUNERKADA considers this engagement as the core of pedagogy and encourages intimate, creative and critical engagement amongst students, teachers and society by promoting healthy enquiries & honest responses.

We would like to see our HUNERMANDS engaged in meaningful & productive dialogue that complements education, trade, industry, art and culture by lending their skills, knowledge, energy and vision to environment and society as agents of change.

New Art & Design Programs

Hunerkada now offers Bachelor of Fine Arts and Bachelor of Design. These programs are tailored to help students become creative professionals and cultural change-makers.

Hunerkada Degree Programs

Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA)

4-Year Degree Programme with specialization in:

  • Paintings
  • Printmaking
  • Miniature Painting
  • Sculpture / 3-D Art

Bachelor of Design (BDes)

4-Year Degree Programme with specialization in:

  • Textile & Fashion Design
  • Ceramics Design
  • Visual Communication Design (Graphic Design)
  • Product Design
  • Interior Design

About Us

University of Swat was established for providing higher education to the people of Swat Valley specifically and the whole of the country generally.

Since its establishment in July 2010, the university has made tremendous progress in academics, research, and infrastructure. Starting with only 7 disciplines, it now offers more than 28 programs.
