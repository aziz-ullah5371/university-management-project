About us

Overview

The College of Home Sciences, established in 2013, is a key part of the Women Campus, University of Swat. It aims to empower women through advanced education in Home Economics, comprising five disciplines: Art & Design, Food & Nutrition, Human Development, Management Studies, and Textile & Clothing.

Inaugurated on 25th July 2017, the Women Campus integrates several departments under the University’s academic and administrative supervision. It supports professional development, leadership, and skill-building among women to impact their communities and the nation positively.

Departments

  • Centre for Animal Sciences & Fisheries
  • Centre for Plant Science and Biodiversity
  • Department of Psychological Studies
  • Department of Home Economics

Our Goals

  • Prepare professionals for community and environmental development.
  • Deliver modern education and develop responsible citizens.
  • Empower women with professional career opportunities.
  • Highlight women’s role as nation builders.
  • Expose students to modern knowledge and technology.
  • Educate women for effective family and social roles.
  • Develop leaders in all fields of Home Economics.
  • Improve quality of life and family strength in Pakistan.
  • Offer excellent scientific psychology education.
  • Foster research-based professional training.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary learning and wide-ranging services.
  • Inspire students to address national and environmental challenges.

About Us

University of Swat was established for providing higher education to the people of Swat Valley specifically and the whole of the country generally.

Since its establishment in July 2010, the university has made tremendous progress in academics, research, and infrastructure. Starting with only 7 disciplines, it now offers more than 28 programs.
